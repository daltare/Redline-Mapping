NOTE: CalEPA regulatory from the Regulated Sites Portal goes in this folder (I haven't yet figured out a way to get an automated update of this data) - the portal is at:
https://siteportal.calepa.ca.gov/nsite/map/export

Download and unzip the following datasets:
- Site Regulated Programs (file = SiteEI.csv )
- Evaluations (file = Evaluations.csv) # this is the inspections data
- Violations (file = Violations.csv)
- Enforcements (file = EA.csv)
